FAQ

Common questions about Lantern Enterprises, our divisions, and how we work.

Is Lantern Advisory a law firm?

No. Lantern Advisory is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation or legal advice. It provides litigation support, discovery support, document organization, research support, and information-structuring services.

Can Lantern Advisory work with my attorney?

Yes. Lantern Advisory can support attorneys, legal teams, consultants, and clients under attorney supervision where applicable. Final legal strategy, legal positions, objections, filings, and legal advice remain with licensed counsel.

Do you provide onsite technology support?

Yes. Lantern Systems can support onsite needs depending on scope, geography, staffing, urgency, and engagement model.

Do you provide remote support?

Yes. Many Lantern Systems, Lantern Advisory, Lantern Research, Freedom Studio, and Threadline-related engagements can be supported remotely.

Do you support executives and family offices?

Yes. Lantern Systems is designed for white-glove executive, enterprise, and family office technology environments requiring discretion, responsiveness, and continuity.

Can Freedom Studio help with ghostwriting?

Yes. Freedom Studio supports ghostwriting, thought leadership, founder stories, website copy, speeches, op-eds, essays, and long-form writing.

Can Freedom Productions support events and video?

Yes. Freedom Productions supports strategic communications production, town halls, corporate events, civic productions, livestreams, founder videos, brand storytelling, and legacy story projects.

What is Lantern Research?

Lantern Research provides research, records review, chronologies, due diligence support, market intelligence, executive briefings, and information synthesis.

What is Threadline Intelligence?

Threadline Intelligence is being developed as an operational memory and knowledge intelligence platform to help people and organizations find what matters, understand what connects, and act with confidence.

Can Threadline be deployed privately?

The long-term vision includes cloud, private cloud, and on-premise deployment models depending on client needs, security requirements, and scale.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on scope, complexity, urgency, staffing, deliverables, access level, onsite needs, confidentiality requirements, and timeline.

Do you offer custom engagements?

Yes. Many clients may need a combination of services such as Advisory + Research, Systems + Threadline, Studio + Productions, or Research + Studio.
