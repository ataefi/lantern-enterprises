About Lantern Advisory

Lantern Advisory helps legal professionals, organizations, consultants, and complex matters bring order to discovery-heavy and information-intensive work.

Many cases become difficult not because the information does not exist, but because it is scattered across records, correspondence, productions, timelines, notes, responses, exhibits, and conflicting narratives.

Lantern Advisory supports the organization, preparation, analysis, and presentation of that information so attorneys and legal teams can focus more effectively on litigation, strategy, advocacy, and client representation.

Lantern Advisory is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation or legal advice. Attorney-supervised work remains under the direction and supervision of licensed counsel.

What We Do

Discovery Strategy Support

We help organize and structure discovery work based on the matter materials, pleadings, known facts, case themes, and attorney direction.

  • Requests for Admission support
  • Requests for Production support
  • Special interrogatory support
  • Form interrogatory organization
  • Issue-based discovery mapping
  • Discovery sequencing and tracking

Discovery Response Support

We help convert client-provided information into clear, organized, professionally structured response drafts for attorney review.

  • Response organization
  • Draft response formatting
  • Objection framework support for attorney review
  • Fact integration from client-provided answers
  • Consistency review across responses
  • Supporting document identification

All legal objections, final positions, and response approvals must be reviewed and approved by licensed counsel where applicable.

Meet-and-Confer Support

We help analyze discovery disputes and organize meet-and-confer correspondence so issues are clear, documented, and professionally presented.

  • Issue summaries
  • Deficiency tracking
  • Response comparison charts
  • Meet-and-confer letter drafting support
  • Supplemental response tracking
  • Exhibit and attachment organization

Chronologies, Evidence & Records Organization

We help transform scattered records into usable timelines, issue lists, and organized evidence sets.

  • Medical record chronology
  • Event timeline development
  • Document indexing
  • Production review support
  • Evidence binders
  • Key document summaries
  • Witness/event cross-references

Deposition Support

We support deposition preparation through organization, exhibits, timelines, summaries, and materials management.

  • Deposition exhibit organization
  • Witness timeline summaries
  • Question-topic organization based on attorney direction
  • Record packets
  • Prior testimony summary support
  • Issue tracking before and after deposition

Lantern Advisory does not provide legal strategy or questioning advice. Deposition strategy remains with licensed counsel.

Trial & Hearing Support

We assist with organization and preparation of matter materials for hearings, mediation, settlement conferences, and trial support.

  • Exhibit organization
  • Timeline refinement
  • Document lists
  • Witness material organization
  • Hearing binder support
  • Trial exhibit coordination
  • Issue summary packets

Engagement Options

Discovery Project

For focused discovery needs such as RFAs, RFPs, interrogatories, responses, or meet-and-confer support.

Discovery Audit

For matters where discovery has already been served or received and the team needs help identifying gaps, risks, inconsistencies, and opportunities for better organization.

Litigation Support Retainer

For ongoing matters requiring recurring discovery support, chronology updates, evidence organization, drafting assistance, and matter management support.

Deposition or Trial Readiness Support

For attorneys and teams preparing for depositions, hearings, settlement conferences, mediation, or trial support.
