Pricing Models

Preliminary pricing architecture for scoped projects, retainers, fractional support, enterprise services, and custom engagements.

Pricing is provided as a planning framework and may vary based on complexity, urgency, staffing, sensitivity, turnaround time, onsite needs, data volume, and client requirements.

Lantern Enterprises does not offer unlimited support. Every engagement should include clear scope, defined deliverables, response expectations, exclusions, and overage terms.
